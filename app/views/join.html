JOIN

SCU ACM SIGGRAPH is more than an organization dedicated to computer graphics. It's a community of passionate indivduals who love all sorts of things.

We blend the technical with the artistic.

As a member of SCU ACM SIGGRAPH, you will join a community of passionate individuals. 3D animation, video game programming and technology, graphic design, post production work, and even data visualization - you can think it, we got you covered. If you're passionate about any one of these topics, you'll feel at home.

> OUR EVENTS

LEARN

The world of computer graphics is rich will technology, and there are many things that may interest you. But, it can be hard to learn what you need to move forward. At SCU ACM SIGGRAPH, we plan workshops and tutorials so that technology isn't foreign.

Software Tutorials

We are planning sessions for you to get comfortable with software like Photoshop and Maya - tools essential to anyone interested in computer graphics. We are also exploring post-production software.

Graphics Programming Workshops

Graphics Programming is an awesome way to get involved with graphics digitally. From OpenGL to more high-level languages like R, learn how to integrate code to create what you want.

Game Engine Bootcamps

How do we make games? Game engines, of course! Learn you're favorite platforms, like Unity, and other open-source engines.

EXPLORE

At SCU ACM SIGGRAPH, we want to explore computer graphics as much as possible. Since it's such a big catagory of computing and art, there is a lot of directions we can go - and at SCU ACM SIGGRAPH, we want to be a resouce for you. For some, it's about getting exposed to our creative side. For dedicated individuals, it's about breaking into the industry - learning what it takes to land a job at studios like Pixar, or game company like Electronic Arts. Either way, we want you to join our community to help you find your passions.

Company and Studio Tours

We are planning tours at many different places to expose you to how it's like to do things professionally.

Industry Speakers

We want to bring as many people as we can to help share what it's like to work in computer graphics. Besides being a great networking opportunity, you will learn a lot, and it's awesome to meet new people.

Conferences

In the heart of Silicon Valley, there are hundreds of conferences related to computer graphics every year. We can go as a group and experience them together - with student discounts! VR, GDC (Game Developers Conference), and a few others are ones that stand out.

COLLABORATE

At the center of our organization is our sense of community. We want to be a club for people with similar interests. That means that SCU ACM SIGGRAPH can server as a catalyst for creation.

Projects

Using the skills of our members, we can collaborate together and create something truly awesome. Chances are, you'll find someone with a particular skill set you need - and vice versa.

Study Together

Being a member means you have access to other students on campus. Work together on school work. We have both Engineers and Artists in our club.

Share Interests

It's hard to find those who share your interests, espcially in computer graphics. When you join, you'll be surrounded by others who are passionate about the same things as you.

What are you waiting for?

Join now! Shoot us an email to let us know your interested, and we will be more than happy to add you to our mailing list!
