JOIN

SCU ACM SIGGRAPH is more than an organization dedicated to computer graphics. It's a community of passionate indivduals who love all sorts of things.

We blend the technical with the artistic.

As a member of SCU ACM SIGGRAPH, you'll join a community dedicated to computer graphics. 3D animation, video games, pre/post production work, and even data visualization - you can think it, we got you covered. If you're passionate about any one of these topics, you'll feel at home.

LEARN

The world of computer graphics is rich will technology, and there are many things that may interest you. But, it can be hard to learn what you need to move forward. At SCU ACM SIGGRAPH, we plan workshops and tutorials so that technology isn't foreign.

We also know that computer graphics isn't all about technology. For dedicated individuals, it's about breaking into the industry - learning what it's like to actually animate for Pixar, or understanding what you need to get recruited at Dreamworks.
